NP Membership

No Fee — Non-Profit Organisations

Designed for registered non-profit organisations committed to serving the Lusaka community. This level gives civil society a platform within the chamber network.

Benefits Include:

  • Chamber member listing in the online directory
  • Attendance at general networking events
  • Access to monthly chamber newsletter
  • Participation in community outreach programmes
  • Voting rights at the Annual General Meeting
🥉

Bronze Membership

K 2,400 / year

Ideal for startups and small businesses looking to grow their local network and gain visibility within Lusaka's commercial ecosystem.

Benefits Include:

  • All NP level benefits
  • Featured in the printed chamber business directory
  • Discounted rates on chamber training workshops
  • 10% discount on chamber event tickets
  • Access to the member resource portal
  • One free classified listing per quarter
🥈

Silver Membership

K 6,000 / year

Suited for growing SMEs that want greater visibility, networking opportunities, and a competitive edge in the Lusaka market.

Benefits Include:

  • All Bronze level benefits
  • Spotlight feature on the chamber website home page (rotational)
  • Priority seating at chamber events and galas
  • 25% discount on all chamber events and training
  • Logo placement in the monthly chamber newsletter
  • One free banner advertisement per year on the website
  • Access to exclusive Silver/Gold member networking dinners
🥇

Gold Membership

K 12,000 / year

The premium tier for established organisations that want maximum exposure, influence, and access to the chamber's full suite of services.

Benefits Include:

  • All Silver level benefits
  • Permanent spotlight position on the chamber home page
  • Dedicated profile page on the chamber website
  • Free exhibition booth at the annual Lusaka Trade & Investment Expo
  • 50% discount on all events and training programmes
  • Speaking opportunity at one chamber event per year
  • Seat on the Chamber Advisory Council
  • Priority referrals and introductions to investors and government contacts
  • Quarterly one-on-one strategic session with chamber leadership
