EduStar was built on a simple belief: every student in Africa deserves access to quality learning resources, regardless of where they live or what they can afford.

Mission

Bridging the
education gap

Millions of students across Africa prepare for critical national examinations each year — often without access to reliable textbooks, professional tutors, or quality study materials. EduStar exists to change that.

By combining a curated library of free, legal textbooks with AI-powered lessons and a peer-learning community, EduStar empowers students to take control of their education — at no cost.
